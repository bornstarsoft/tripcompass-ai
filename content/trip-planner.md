TripCompass AI is designed to plan after the destination choice is clear. A useful short-trip plan should reduce backtracking, keep travel days realistic, and separate must-book items from flexible ideas.

## Planning Flow

1. Choose a destination shortlist.
2. Pick the trip length and arrival time.
3. Group activities by area.
4. Check hotels, flights, activities, transit, and eSIM needs.
5. Verify opening hours, booking terms, and local rules before paying.
