This sample 3 day Tokyo itinerary groups each day by area so the trip does not become a train-map puzzle.

## Day 1: Arrival, Shibuya, and Harajuku

- Arrive, check transit cards or mobile payment setup, and drop bags.
- Walk Shibuya Crossing, Miyashita Park, and nearby shopping streets.
- Add Harajuku or Omotesando if energy is high.
- Keep dinner near the hotel or the final neighborhood.

## Day 2: Asakusa, Ueno, and Akihabara

- Start early in Asakusa.
- Move to Ueno for museums, parks, or casual food.
- End in Akihabara if electronics, games, or anime shops are a priority.

## Day 3: Ginza, Tokyo Station, or a Flexible Favorite

- Use the final full day for Ginza, Tsukiji area, Tokyo Station, or a missed neighborhood.
- Keep one open block for shopping, cafes, or a weather backup.
- Confirm airport timing before adding late activities.
