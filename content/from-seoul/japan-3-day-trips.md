For a 3 day Japan trip from Seoul, the strongest early candidates are Fukuoka, Osaka, and Tokyo. The right choice depends on pace and purpose.

## Best Overall: Fukuoka

Fukuoka is compact, easy to navigate, and strong for food. It is a good choice when the goal is a relaxed short trip rather than a packed checklist.

## Best Variety: Tokyo

Tokyo works for 3 days if you choose a few neighborhoods and avoid trying to see everything. It is best for shopping, pop culture, dining variety, and first-time excitement.

## Best Food Plus Day Trips: Osaka

Osaka can work in 3 days, especially for food and nightlife. It becomes stronger with 4 days if Kyoto or Nara is part of the plan.
