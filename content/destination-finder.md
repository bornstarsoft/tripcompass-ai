TripCompass AI starts with the destination decision. The goal is to help travelers narrow a broad idea like "three or four days from Seoul" into a short list of destinations worth comparing.

## What Phase 1 Includes

- A static finder form on the homepage.
- Sample recommendation cards for Fukuoka, Osaka, Tokyo, and Da Nang.
- Booking/search CTA links routed through `/go/...` paths.
- Clear reminders to verify details before booking.

## What Comes Later

The future AI version will return structured recommendations using travel preferences, constraints, and fallback mock results. Phase 1 does not call OpenAI and does not claim live prices, availability, or official rules.

- First-time 4 day Japan trip with easy transit.
- Family beach trip for 4 days.
