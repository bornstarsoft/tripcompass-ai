# TripCompass AI — Project Brief

## Brand
TripCompass AI

## Domains
- Main: https://tripcompass.ai
- Redirect: https://tripcompassai.com -> https://tripcompass.ai

## One-line positioning
TripCompass AI is an AI destination finder, trip planner, and travel decision engine.

## Tagline
Find where to go, plan what to do, compare what to book.

## Core concept
TripCompass AI should not be just another AI itinerary generator. It should help users decide where to go first, compare destination options, create a practical itinerary, and then continue to trusted booking/search partners.

## Primary target
Korea-based travelers planning short international trips, especially:
- Japan
- Southeast Asia
- Taiwan / Hong Kong
- Guam / Saipan

## Initial languages
- English as the main language
- Korean section under /ko/

## Main user questions
- Where should I go for a 3-4 day trip from Seoul?
- Tokyo vs Osaka: which is better for first-time travelers?
- Fukuoka vs Osaka: which is better for a short food trip?
- Da Nang vs Bangkok: which is better for families?
- What itinerary should I follow after choosing a destination?
- What should I check before booking hotels, flights, activities, or eSIM?

## MVP services
1. AI Destination Finder
2. Destination Compare pages
3. AI Trip Planner
4. Booking/search link router through /go/...
5. Email/save later, optional in later phase
6. Korean /ko/ pages, starting small

## Business model
Initial model:
- Free AI destination recommendation
- Free trip planning suggestions
- Booking/search CTA clicks
- Affiliate-ready link router
- eSIM, activities, hotels, flights later
- Click data for future affiliate applications

Later model:
- Travel affiliate revenue
- Premium PDF Trip Kit
- B2B AI trip planning widget
- Travel agency/local tour lead generation

## Important strategic rule
The site must work even before direct affiliate approvals. Booking/search buttons should exist from the beginning, but should route through /go/... so links can be changed later without editing content pages.

## Safety and trust
TripCompass AI must not claim:
- real-time lowest prices
- guaranteed availability
- confirmed visa rules
- confirmed opening hours
- final safety/legal/medical advice

Every AI result should include this kind of disclaimer:
TripCompass AI provides planning suggestions. Always check current prices, opening hours, visa rules, availability, and booking terms before booking.
